Great Divide Mountain Bike Route
The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route runs from Banff, AB to Antelope Wells, NM. Total mileage: 2768.4 miles
Dates: June 12-19, 2018
Costs: $300 per day or $2000 for the entire trip
Basic requirements: Must be an experienced cyclist. Must provide your own snacks and water. Shuttles are available at extra cost.
